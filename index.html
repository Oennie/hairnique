Hair Tips

Know your hair

Real education for real natural hair. No gatekeeping here.

🤹
Understanding Your Hair
Whether your hair is coily, curly, wavy or somewhere in between — consistent moisture, gentle handling and the right products make all the difference.
Key things to know
Every hair type has its own unique curl pattern and care needs
Shrinkage is normal — your hair may be much longer than it looks
Moisture retention is the foundation of healthy, thriving hair
It thrives with gentle manipulation and protective styles
Wide-tooth combs and detangling brushes are your best friends
💧
The LOC Method
The LOC method (Liquid, Oil, Cream) is the gold standard for moisturising natural hair. Apply in order for maximum moisture retention and softer, more manageable hair.
L — Liquid (water or leave-in conditioner)
O — Oil (seal the moisture in)
C — Cream (thick butter to lock everything in)
🌿
Growing Natural Hair
Natural hair grows at the same rate as any other hair type — the key is retaining length. Protective styles, minimal manipulation and consistent moisturising are the real secrets to long, flourishing hair.
⛔
Ingredients to Avoid
Some common ingredients can actually dry out or damage your hair. Watch out for: sulphates (in shampoos), mineral oil, petrolatum and alcohol-heavy products. Always read the label.
